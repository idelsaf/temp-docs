# Quick Start

Welcome to TaskTracker! This guide will help you create your first task in just a few minutes.

## 1. Creating an Account

Navigate to the [application website](https://app.tasktracker.example.com) and click **"Sign Up"**.

!!! tip "Tip"
    Use a valid email address to receive important notifications about your tasks.

## 2. Creating Your First Task

After logging in:

1. Click the **"New Task"** button in the top navigation bar.
2. Fill in the required fields:
    - **Title**: "Review project requirements"
    - **Description**: "Collect and document requirements for the new module"
    - **Assignee**: Select yourself from the dropdown list.
3. Click **"Create"** to save your task.

## 3. Next Steps

Now that you've created your first task, you can:

- [ ] Explore the full guide on [working with tasks](./tasks.md)
- [ ] Learn about [API capabilities](../api/overview.md) for automation
- [ ] Customize your notification preferences in Settings
